About Me

I build with the same care I'd want as a user.

I'm a junior web developer from Phnom Penh, Cambodia, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Information Technology at the National University of Management while completing an intensive 700-hour scholarship program at ANT Technology Training Center provided by the Ministry of Post and Telecommunications (MPTC). My toolkit centers on Vue.js for interfaces and Node.js / Express.js with MySQL underneath — but what I care about most is whether the end result actually feels good to use.

I've built responsive interfaces and full-stack features across two team projects, from authentication flows to RESTful APIs and CRUD operations. I like clean systems, thoughtful edge cases, and interfaces that don't need an explanation.
